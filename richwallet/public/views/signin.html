RichWallet

Secure DIY wallet service for multiple coins, such as bitcoin, litecoin, dogecoin

  • Secure Your wallet data is encrypted, nobody can steal it, even if our service is down, you can restore your wallet and all the money using your backup.
  • Rich USD and Euro can be put within one wallet since they are pieces of sheet. Here, we can put BTC, LTC and any other cryptcoins in one single wallet.
  • Easy Receive, send coins with a few mouse clicks.
  • Portible Access your wallet via iPhone, Android phone or PC.
  • Open Richwallet is an open source service, please feel free to use it and contrib to it. Richwallet is also benifit from other open source project coinpunk thanks to open source community.
